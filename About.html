Who We Are

First Daystar Analytical Laboratory Company is a registered analytical laboratory under the Institute of Public Analysts of Nigeria (IPAN), with IPAN number 034. It was established in August 2016.

Our analytical results are recognized by both the National Agency for Food and Drug Administration and Control (NAFDAC) and the Standards Organization of Nigeria (SON).

We are committed to conducting accurate and thorough investigations, particularly supporting research students and industries across Nigeria. Our services cover testing of:

  • Food
  • Drugs
  • Water
  • Soil
  • Herbs

Our trained personnel meticulously analyze for chemicals, heavy metals, aflatoxins, pesticides, vitamins, and microbiological content, ensuring reliable and internationally comparable results.
